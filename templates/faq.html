Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Klevaedge

Getting Started

How do I create an account?
Click Get Started on the homepage, enter your name, email and password. Your account is ready immediately — you can deposit and start trading right away.
Is there a minimum deposit?
Yes, the minimum deposit is $10 USD equivalent in any supported cryptocurrency.

Deposits & Withdrawals

How long do deposits take?
Deposits are credited after blockchain confirmations: Bitcoin (~30 min), Ethereum (~3 min), USDT (varies), Litecoin (~15 min). Once confirmed and approved by our team your balance updates instantly.
How long do withdrawals take?
Withdrawals are typically processed within 24–48 hours. Once approved, delivery time depends on network speed.
Are there any fees?
Klevaedge does not charge deposit fees. Withdrawal fees vary by cryptocurrency and network conditions — the exact fee is shown before you confirm.

Security & Safety

Is Klevaedge safe?
Yes. We use SSL encryption, secure password hashing, and regular security reviews to protect your account and funds at all times.
How do I keep my account secure?
Use a strong unique password and never share your credentials. Always verify you are on the official Klevaedge website before entering any information.

Trading & Profits

How do I make profits?
Profits are generated through our expert trading strategies, copy trading, staking, and subscription plans. You can track real-time growth directly on your dashboard.
Can I withdraw my profits anytime?
Yes — you can withdraw your full balance including profits at any time, subject to the minimum withdrawal amount of $10.
